## What is FounderCalc?

FounderCalc is a set of free, instant business calculators built for the same
people who built it: indie founders, freelancers, and early-stage SaaS
builders who need fast, accurate answers without signing up for a dashboard
or wading through a spreadsheet.

Every calculator runs **entirely in your browser**. When you enter numbers
into any tool on this site, those numbers never leave your device.

## The tools

The current calculator suite covers the questions that come up constantly
when you're running a small digital business:

- **Payments** — Stripe fee calculator so you know exactly what you receive.
- **SaaS metrics** — MRR, ARR, LTV:CAC ratio, startup runway, and churn impact.
- **Freelance** — minimum hourly rate based on income targets and tax, salary-to-hourly conversion.
- **Business math** — profit margins, break-even analysis, VAT/sales tax.

## Why we built this

These calculations aren't hard, but doing them from scratch in a spreadsheet
every time is friction that slows decisions. A single-purpose calculator that
opens instantly, pre-filled with sensible defaults, gets you an answer in 10
seconds.

## No tracking, no sign-up

All computation is client-side JavaScript. There is no account, no email
required, and no analytics tracking what you type. The only data that leaves
your browser is a standard server request for the HTML page itself.

If you have a suggestion for a calculator that would be useful, you can reach
us via the [contact page](/contact/).
