## Get in touch

FreeToolKit is a small, independently run site. If you've found a bug, have a
suggestion for a new tool, or have a question about how something works,
we'd like to hear from you.

**Email:** [{{ contact_email }}](mailto:{{ contact_email }})

When reporting a bug, it helps to include:

- The tool you were using and a link to its page
- What you expected to happen
- What happened instead
- Your browser and operating system

We read every message, though as a small site we may not be able to respond
to every one individually.
