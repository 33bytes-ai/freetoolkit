## Stripe vs PayPal Fees — Which Costs Less?

Type a sale amount into the calculator above and it instantly shows the fee and net
payout for **Stripe**, **PayPal Standard**, **PayPal Checkout**, and **Shopify Payments**
on that transaction, sorted from most to least you keep.

## Stripe vs PayPal at a glance

| | Stripe | PayPal Standard | PayPal Checkout |
|---|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Domestic card rate | 2.9% + $0.30 | 2.99% + $0.49 | 3.49% + $0.49 |
| International card | 4.4% + $0.30 | 4.49% + $0.49 | 4.99% + $0.49 |
| Monthly fee | $0 | $0 | $0 |
| Payout speed | 2 business days | Instant to balance, bank withdrawal 1–3 days | Instant to balance, bank withdrawal 1–3 days |
| Best for | SaaS, APIs, B2B invoicing | Consumer trust, marketplaces | Express checkout button conversions |

On a **$100 domestic card sale**, Stripe takes $3.20 and you keep $96.80. PayPal
Standard takes $3.48 ($96.52 net) — $0.28 more than Stripe. PayPal Checkout takes
$3.98 ($96.02 net), the most expensive of the three.

## Why PayPal is usually more expensive than Stripe

PayPal's per-transaction fixed fee ($0.49) is higher than Stripe's ($0.30), and its
percentage rate is also higher on every tier. The gap widens on smaller transactions,
where the fixed fee dominates the total cost. On a $10 sale, Stripe takes $0.59 (5.9%
effective rate) versus PayPal Standard's $0.79 (7.9% effective rate).

PayPal can still be worth the extra cost: roughly 60% of US online shoppers have a
PayPal account, and offering it as a checkout option measurably reduces cart
abandonment for consumer-facing stores. Use the calculator above to see whether the
conversion lift is likely to outweigh the fee difference for your average order size.

## Where Shopify Payments fits in

If you sell through Shopify, Shopify Payments usually beats both Stripe and PayPal
on domestic card rates (as low as 1.5% + $0.25 on the Advanced plan) and avoids the
0.5–2% third-party gateway surcharge Shopify otherwise charges for using Stripe or
PayPal directly. Run your own numbers with the [Shopify Fee Calculator](/tools/shopify-fee-calculator/).
