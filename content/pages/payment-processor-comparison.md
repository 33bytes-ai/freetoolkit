## Stripe vs PayPal vs Shopify Payments — Which Is Cheapest?

Choosing the wrong payment processor costs real money. This comparison covers
the three most common choices for online businesses: **Stripe**, **PayPal**, and
**Shopify Payments**.

## Which should you choose?

### Choose Stripe if:
- You're building a **SaaS product** — Stripe Billing is the best subscription infrastructure available
- You need **developer-grade APIs** — Stripe's webhook reliability, SDK quality, and documentation are unmatched
- You want **global coverage** — Stripe supports 135+ currencies and 40+ countries natively
- Your customers are **B2B** — Stripe's invoicing, payment links, and ACH support are superior

### Choose PayPal if:
- A large share of your customers **prefer PayPal** — 60% of US online shoppers have a PayPal account and trust it
- You're selling on **marketplaces** (eBay, Etsy) where PayPal is the expected method
- You need **Venmo integration** for younger consumer audiences

### Choose Shopify Payments if:
- You're already on **Shopify** — it eliminates the 0.5–2% third-party gateway fee that Shopify charges for using Stripe directly, making it cheaper than Stripe in most cases
- Your store is **US-based, consumer-focused** — the rate is genuinely lower than Stripe for domestic Visa/Mastercard
- You want the **simplest setup** — no separate account, disputes handled in Shopify admin

## What the fee comparison misses

**Chargeback rates**: PayPal has historically higher chargeback rates than Stripe, especially for digital products. Factor in dispute resolution time when choosing.

**Payout speed**: Stripe pays out in 2 business days (or same-day with Instant Payouts at 1.5% fee). PayPal holds funds for longer for new accounts (up to 21 days) and can freeze accounts with less notice than Stripe.

**Subscription support**: Stripe Billing is significantly better than PayPal's subscription APIs for SaaS — better webhook reliability, proration, trial management, and plan switching.

**Tax compliance**: Stripe Tax (add-on) and Paddle (merchant of record) are better options than PayPal for international VAT compliance.
